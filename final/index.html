Flashcards

How to Use:

  1. Click on each flash card to flip them.
  2. Add Cards by filling in the textboxes and remove cards by clicking on the 'X' on the top-right corner on the front of each card.
  3. Each card can be rated on a scale of easy, medium, and hard by flipping it to its back.
  4. Press the 'Test' button below to take a randomized test on the flashcards you have entered.
    • Cards that have not been rated will be presented first.
    • There's a 50% probability that the card presented will have a rating of 'Hard,' a 30% probabiltiy that it will have a rating of 'Medium,' and a 20% probability that it will have a rating of 'Easy.'
